About

Kalayaka is a space inspired by yoga’s central idea of union—bringing together what’s scattered, and remembering what’s already whole.

This site will grow over time with short reflections, simple practices, and links to resources.

Breath

Return to the inhale and exhale. Let breath be the bridge back to now.

Body

Move with care. Build steadiness, mobility, and strength—without forcing.

Awareness

Notice. Soften. Choose again. Union begins with attention.
